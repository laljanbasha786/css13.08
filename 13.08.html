Prabhas is an Indian actor who predominantly works in Telugu cinema.

Prabhas played a dual role in S.S. Rajamouli's epic action film Baahubali.

Prabhas went on to star in Saaho, Radhe Shyam & Adipurush, none of which performed well commercially.
